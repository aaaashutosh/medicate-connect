Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book appointments through your patient dashboard by clicking "Book Consultation" and selecting your preferred doctor and time slot.

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and comply with HIPAA regulations to ensure your health information is completely secure and private.

How does the AI symptom checker work?

Our AI analyzes your symptoms using machine learning algorithms trained on medical databases to provide preliminary assessments and recommendations.

Can I access my prescriptions online?

Yes, all your prescriptions are digitally stored in your patient dashboard where you can view, download, and request refills easily.
